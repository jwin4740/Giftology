Giftology is a simple to use, gift management app, that will help you keep track of the people you care. You will be able to search gift ideas, assign them to your contacts and share lists with family and friends.

Find Gifts

Gift Management

Add an idea whenever inspiration comes. Giftology lets you save a gift idea to your list or assign to one of your contacts.

Contacts Library

Giftology keeps your gift recipients' details all in one place. You can keep up with their birthday, mailing address, and gift lists.

Share Lists

It's easy to share lists with Giftology. Simply select a contact and click "share list." Then choose a recipient to share gift information with!
